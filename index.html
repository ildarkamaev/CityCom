    • CityCom

    Making Communication between citizens and public servants easier

    What?

    CityCom is a modern solution for community involvement in city planning process. We provide solution for problem reporting from the citizens side and perspective workflow mapping for municipalities. By auto-prioritizion facility we make public servants` job more productive and citizen-focused.

    Why?

  • To make civil servants work more effective and improve communication between civil servants and citizens
  • Helps municipalities to make better and transparent decisions
  • Reduce bureaucracy
  • Raise citizens satisfaction with the city

    • How?

    Our solution shows actual work of responsible public servants, motivates them to perform the job better. By seeing and realizing that there is an actual improvement made local community will overcome the friction and be more active in co-working with municipality.

    We help in improving city infrastructure (including public transport, car parking problems, roads locations). We provide visual reports.

    We support local community activism, connect people and municipality with common aim of making a better living environment in the city. We add transparency to the everyday work of municipal workers.
